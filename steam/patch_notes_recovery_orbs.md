# Huebound Update - Recovery Orbs

**A quality-of-life update for those first few minutes after a reset.**

## What's New

**Flying Recovery Orbs**

Ever prestige your entire factory, spend your last Light on a fancy new node, realize you forgot to place a Seller, and find yourself completely broke? Yeah. That happened.

We've added **Flying Recovery Orbs** — wandering color nodes that drift across the canvas when you're in a tight spot. Click one to collect **50 Light** instantly. They appear only when:

- Your currency drops below what a Seller costs
- AND you don't have a working factory (no Seller + no Source = no income)

Once you place a Seller and a Source, or save up enough to buy one, the orbs vanish. They're training wheels that gracefully bow out once you can stand on your own.

## Why This Matters

Prestige is meant to feel like a fresh start with new tools — not a trap door. The escalating cost curve on Sellers meant that after enough prestiges, a single mistake could leave you with zero options and zero income. That's not a fun puzzle to solve. That's a softlock.

These orbs ensure you always have **one more chance** to get back on your feet without breaking the game's economy. They're help, not a handout.

## Special Thanks

This fix was prompted by feedback from **ParadiddlePride**, who discovered the edge case that made this recovery mechanic necessary. Thanks for finding the outlier — and for caring enough to report it.
